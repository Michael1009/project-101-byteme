SkiMatch

SkiMatch is a web application created at the University of Virginia and designed for use by members of the UVA community. Our platform allows students and faculty to connect over shared skills, interests, experiences and expertise. Users can share a personal bio on their past experiences, list courses they have taken and add in their relevant skills. Search for members of the UVA community with similar interests or common organizations to form study groups, offer tutoring, find a professor conducting research in your area of interest or develop a new hobby.

Let other users know how you intend to use Skimatch by updating your status with what you are looking for -- whether that's tutoring in calculus or a group of students to compete with in a hackathon. If you know another user is great at software development or at playing softball, you can endorse that skill on their profile to let others know they're a good resource for gaining more experience with that topic. There's no limit to the kinds of connections you can make using SkiMatch, so get searching today!
