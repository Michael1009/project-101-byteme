SkiMatch

SkiMatch is a UVA restricted web application modeled after LinkedIn that allows students and faculty to create a profile page and post information about themselves, including what skills/expertise they have. Users can share the classes they have taken and the interests they have as well as the organizations of which they are a part. SkiMatch enables students and faculty to easily search for one another to find others that share similar hobbies and experiences.
